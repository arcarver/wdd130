Annie Carver | WWD 130

"When you go on the river, wear a life jacket. I once had search and rescue called beacuse I tipped over in kayak and my upside down kayak floated down the Provo River. Because I wore my life-jacket I am alive today. I live near Seattle, Washington with my sweet husband and five beautiful children, and a dog. In the summer we swim and hike. In the winter we go into the mountians to find snow and go sledding. Our dog tolerates swimming. He has a lifejacket and paddles as fast as he can towards the shore. He only tolerates the water. In the winter we play a lot of boardgames. My favorite is Ticket to Ride Legacy. On of my children loves to play Five Crowns. They are a card shark. Don't tell my them I told you. They almost always win in card games."
